MEGA MAN STAR FORCE 3: BLACK ACE CHIP DATA

Table starts at 0x000D58CC
(0x000D18CC in arm9)

Byte0: Element and Class
Byte1: Game Exclusivity
Byte2: Traits
Byte3: Effects

Byte4 & Byte5: Portrait
Byte6 & Byte7: Palette
Byte8 & Byte9: Damage

ByteA - ByteF: Star Card Values

== BYTE0 ==

Bit1 nor Bit2: White Card
Bit1: Yellow Card
Bit2: Blue Card
Bit1 and Bit2: Red Card
Bit3 only: Fire
Bit4 only: Aqua
Bit3 and Bit4: Elec
Bit5 only: Wood
Bit3 and Bit5: "F" Icon
Bit4 and Bit5: "*" Icon
Bit3, Bit4, and Bit5: Sword Icon
Bit6: Mega Class
Bit7: Giga Class

== BYTE1 ==

Most Cards are FF
Gigas in Black Ace Library are 00
Gigas in Red Joker Library are 01

== BYTE2 ==

Bit1: Displays Damage & Eats PlusCards
Bit2: Stop Time / Dimming
Bit3: Unused?
Bit4: Blue Text / Illegal
Bit5: Shows up in Megas
Bit6: Interrupt / Counterattack
Bit8: Sword-type

== BYTE3 ==

Bit1: Breaks Guard
Bit2: Ignores I-frames
Bit3: Applies Wind
Bit4: Induces I-frames
Bit5: Flinches
Bit6: Summons EM Body
