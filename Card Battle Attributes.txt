Starting Addresses:

Battle Cards: 0x000D7274
Folder Cards: 0x000D745D

Byte 1 = Variant (High Bits), Power / Rarity Grade (Low Bits)
Byte 2 = Lock-On Methods
Byte 3 = ?
Byte 4 = Effect
Bytes 5 & 6 = Entity Health / EM Body to Summon / Healing Amount
Byte 7 = ? (possibly cooldown?)
Byte 8 = ?

- Power / Rarity Grades come in 0-A
- Changing this does not seem to affect Noise Reward pools
